Frequently Asked Questions

How do I read the traffic map?

The map displays routes color-coded by congestion level: green for light, yellow for moderate, and red for heavy traffic.

What do the traffic light timings mean?

The countdown shows remaining seconds until the next signal change. Each light cycles through red, yellow, and green states.

How often is data updated?

Traffic data refreshes every 30 seconds, while traffic light states update in real-time every second.
